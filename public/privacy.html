PINMAP/ Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective immediately

PINMAP is a social mapping app built and operated by Seth Gray. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it simple because we don't do anything shady.

1. What We Collect

When you use PINMAP we collect:

  • Account info — your name and email address when you sign in with Google
  • Pins — location, name, description, hashtags, photos, and privacy settings you create
  • Comments — text comments and replies you post on pins
  • Follows — hashtags you choose to follow
  • Usage data — general app usage via Cloudflare analytics (no cookies, no personal tracking)

We do not collect precise location data in the background. Location is only used when you explicitly drop a pin or use the nearby feature.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your pins and profile within the app
  • To send you in-app notifications about comments on your pins
  • To provide search and discovery features for public pins
  • To improve the app based on general usage patterns

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

3. Third-Party Services

PINMAP uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Google OAuth — sign-in only; we receive your name and email
  • Cloudflare — hosting and CDN (pin-map.com)
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — map tiles and address search
  • Esri — satellite imagery tiles

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Public vs Private Pins

Pins you mark as public are visible to all users. Pins marked private are only visible to you. Pins marked shareable are accessible via a direct link.

Your username (Google display name) is shown on your public pins and comments.

5. Photos

Photos you attach to pins are stored in Supabase Storage and are publicly accessible via URL if the pin is public. Do not attach photos containing sensitive personal information.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can delete individual pins and comments at any time within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Children

PINMAP is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights contact us at:

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app's What's New feature. Continued use of PINMAP after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
